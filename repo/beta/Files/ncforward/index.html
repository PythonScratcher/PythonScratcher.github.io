Should be compatible with iOS 5.0 - 8.x

NCForward lets you forward your iOS notifications to ANY other device. Currently, Windows and Mac are supported, Linux is not.
You will need to run this program on your Windows computer or this program on your Mac to forward your notifications within your LAN.
To forward your notifications to Pushbullet devices, sign in to Pushbullet in settings.
For further information and documentation read here: NCForward - Forward your notifications
