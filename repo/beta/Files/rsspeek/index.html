Requires iOS 6, might work on 5, 7, 8, 9 as well...

(Notice: This is still public "testing" phase. It works very reliable, but if you find a problem, it is not an intended feature. Email me please!)
An RSS client which does not suck. In your notification center, waiting to serve you! I desigend RSSPeek to integrate as unobstrusive as possible into the "stock" iOS operating system. A second design primitive was a good performance in both time and reliability of serving fast and recent content to the user.

- RSSPeek shows "offline" although a default RSS feed (Apple PR) should be loaded.
